About this property

3150 Dutton Ave is an industrial-zoned parcel in Santa Rosa, ideally positioned as the foundation of the Equitide model. The primary intent is to develop an offsite modular construction facility — a factory that builds the components used in all future Equitide residential projects. This dramatically reduces per-unit build cost and time compared to traditional site construction.

The site will also include a set of experimental rental units built using the offsite method. These serve as proof-of-concept for the construction approach, generate early rental income, and give Equitide a living demonstration of the full model before scaling to larger residential projects.

This is an owner-financed deal. The seller retains tokens representing their equity stake and receives cash as new investors purchase shares. No bank, no interest to a lender — the seller becomes the fund's first patient investor.
