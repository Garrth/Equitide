About Equitide

A new model for real estate investment

Equitide is a vertically integrated real estate trust built to make institutional-grade real estate investment accessible to everyone — not just the wealthy.

By aggregating smaller investors, Equitide secures preferred equity positions that would otherwise require minimum investments of $500,000 or more. The result: better returns for investors, lower costs for developers, and more attainable housing for tenants.

Company Facts

What journalists need to know

Founded by Garth Wells, a Northern California general contractor with over 30 years of experience in construction and real estate development.

Equitide operates as a platform provider for individual eREITs focused on attainable housing development. The platform connects accredited investors with vetted development projects at preferred equity positions.

Headquartered in San Francisco, California. Currently raising for Cedar Mill, a premier attainable housing development project.
