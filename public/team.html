Garth Wells

Founder & CEO

Garth Wells brings more than 30 years of hands-on experience in construction and real estate development to Equitide. A Northern California general contractor who has worked at every level of the development stack, Garth built Equitide after watching the same structural misalignments drive up costs and lock out everyday investors time and again. His answer: a vertically integrated model where incentives are aligned from the ground up — so investors, developers, builders, and tenants all win together. Cedar Mill is his first proof of concept.

Irina Wells

Head of Finance

Irina Wells is a financial executive with over two decades of experience in construction, real estate development, and commercial property — making her an ideal fit for Equitide's mission to bring institutional discipline to attainable housing investment. As Controller at HL Commercial Real Estate and former Controller/CFO at Monahan Pacific Corporation, Irina has managed the full financial lifecycle of complex real estate operations, from budgets-to-actuals reconciliation and investor reporting to auditing, payroll, and job cost analysis. Irina holds an MBA from Colorado Technical University and a BA in Business Administration from Golden Gate University.

Michael Morawski

Sales Advisor

Mike Morawski is a 30-year real estate investment veteran who has overseen more than $285 million in real estate transactions. An entrepreneur, author, trainer, and public speaker, Mike brings deep multifamily expertise and a proven track record of building investor communities to Equitide's go-to-market strategy. In 2005, Mike founded a private equity firm, raising $18 million in private capital to acquire $60 million in multifamily apartments — more than 4,000 units across five U.S. markets. Today Mike channels that experience into coaching and training real estate investors through My Core Intentions. He is the host of the Insider Secrets Podcast and co-host of the Multifamily Unplugged Vidcast.

Dana Constance

Founding Creative Director

Dana Constance is an award-winning creative director and brand strategist with deep roots in both corporate communications and purpose-driven design. As Founding Creative Director of Equitide, Dana leads the visual identity, storytelling, and marketing strategy behind the company's mission to make real estate investment accessible to everyone. He brings to Equitide the same instinct that has defined his career — finding the human story inside complex systems and translating it into design and language that moves people to act. His work spans brand identity, digital experience, investor communications, and campaign strategy, giving Equitide a creative foundation built to earn trust as much as attention.
