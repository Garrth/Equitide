Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

Overview

Equitide ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website equitide.io. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that information. We keep this simple because we keep your data simple.

Information we collect

Information you provide directly. When you join our waitlist, we collect your first name and email address, and whether you identify as an accredited or non-accredited investor. We do not collect payment information, social security numbers, or financial data through this website at this time.

Information collected automatically. Like most websites, we use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use the site. This may include your IP address, browser type, pages visited, and time spent on the site. This data is aggregated and anonymized — we use it to improve the site, not to identify you personally. We also use cookies necessary for the site to function correctly.

How we use your information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Notify you when investment opportunities become available
  • Communicate updates about Equitide and our properties
  • Understand how people use our website so we can improve it

How we share your information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties.

Mailchimp. Waitlist signups are stored and managed through Mailchimp, our email service provider. Mailchimp processes your name and email address on our behalf and is bound by their own privacy policy.

Google Analytics. Anonymized site usage data is shared with Google Analytics as described above.

Legal and regulatory requirements. If you become an investor, certain transactions may be subject to mandatory reporting requirements under applicable law. For example, cash transactions over $10,000 may be required to be reported to the Financial Crimes Enforcement Network (FinCEN) under federal law. We will comply with all applicable legal and regulatory reporting obligations.

Cookies

We use cookies for basic site functionality and analytics. When you visit equitide.io, a cookie notice will give you the option to accept or decline non-essential cookies. Functional cookies required for the site to operate cannot be disabled.

Your rights

You may request to access, correct, or delete the personal information we hold about you at any time. To make a request, contact us at meridian@equitide.io. We will respond within a reasonable time.

You may unsubscribe from our email list at any time using the unsubscribe link in any email we send.

Data security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as our practices evolve, particularly as we begin accepting investments and additional regulatory requirements apply. We will update the date at the top of this page when changes are made.

Contact

Questions about this policy? Email us at meridian@equitide.io.
